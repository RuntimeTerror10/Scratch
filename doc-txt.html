Text Alignment is an effective web design principle which allows us to make conscious decisions about how and where elements are placed on the page.

Instead of creating another class you can use "txt-left" , "txt-center" , "txt-right" to align text in your webpage.

You can also use "txt-decoration-none" to remove the underline from the anchor links.
