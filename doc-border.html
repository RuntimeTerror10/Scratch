Borders

Highlight your webpage components by adding borders around them.

Border-radius

Add classes to an element to easily round it's corners.

<div class=".... rounded"><div>

<div class=".... rounded-top"><div>

<div class=".... rounded-bottom"><div>

<div class=".... rounded-right"><div>

<div class=".... rounded-left"><div>

<div class=".... rounded-circle"><div>

<div class=".... rounded-pill"><div>

Border-color

Add colors to an element's border.

<div class=".... rounded border-blue"><div>

<div class=".... rounded-top border-green"><div>

<div class=".... rounded-bottom border-red"><div>

<div class=".... rounded-right border-yellow"><div>

<div class=".... rounded-left border-bright"><div>

<div class=".... rounded-circle border-dark"><div>

You can also add "border-violet" and "border-orange" or you can create another class to add your own color.

Border-style

Add different styles of borders on elements as shown below.

<div class=".... rounded border-blue border-dotted"><div>

<div class=".... rounded-top border-green border-dash"><div>

<div class=".... rounded-bottom border-red border-double"><div>

<div class=".... rounded-right border-yellow border-solid"><div>

<div class=".... rounded-left border-bright border-ridge"><div>

<div class=".... rounded-circle border-dark border-outset"><div>
